About UmbrellaCorporation

Our Story

Founded in 2020, UmbrellaCorporation began with a simple mission: to make it easy for anyone to start and grow an online business. We saw that existing platforms were either too complex or too limited, leaving many entrepreneurs frustrated.

Today, we serve over 50,000 businesses worldwide, helping them connect with millions of customers. Our team of 100+ professionals is dedicated to continuously improving our platform to meet the evolving needs of modern businesses.

Our Values

  • Customer Success: We measure our success by our customers' success
  • Innovation: We're constantly looking for better ways to solve problems
  • Integrity: We do what we say and say what we do
  • Community: We believe in the power of entrepreneurs helping each other
